Our application is named CAPE that basically means, Cultural Advertisement, Preservation, and Empowement.
It is an online shopping web app that is solely for Filipino cultural products.
Sellers must be fit and follow the criteria in order to become a partner of CAPE.
An additional feature of our app is that it promotes the upcoming festival or important even in our culture.


Open the html file named, "Index.html"



In building the app, we only used HTML, CSS, aBootstrap and Vue JS to present our idea.
